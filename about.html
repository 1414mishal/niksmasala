The Taste of Mangalorean Tradition

The Essence of Mangalorean Cuisine.

At Niks Masala, we bring you the true essence of Mangalorean cuisine — a perfect fusion of authentic flavours passed down through generations. Rooted in the rich culinary heritage of the coastal city of Mangalore, our journey began with a passion to preserve and share its vibrant spice culture.

Every blend is carefully handcrafted using premium-quality ingredients, ensuring that each dish bursts with the freshness, aroma, and taste that define Mangalore's legacy of flavour.

Our Facility

State-of-the-Art Production in Mangalore.

Our factory, located at Yeyyadi, Mangalore, covers a spacious production area of over 11,000 sq. ft. It is equipped with modern machinery and advanced technology, enabling us to produce high-quality, export-standard products with utmost precision and hygiene.

Every stage of our production process is carefully monitored to ensure consistency, safety, and superior taste. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and excellence reflects in every product that leaves our facility — catering to customers who value authentic flavours and premium standards.
